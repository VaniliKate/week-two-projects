Hello There!!

This is the Akan name generator, a web application that will allow you to input you birth details such as date, month and year to find out what your Akan name is.

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.
These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name. Most Ghanaians have at least one name from this system, even if they also have an English or Christian name.
